# &#x1F3A5; GenuTube

The **GenuTube** is one plataform of videos inspired in YouTube, but with one proposal: **watch and share content without numbers**.  
No likes, dislikes our views — just videos and authenticity. (not avaible now!)

---

## &#x1F4DA; About the Project

The GenuTube was born from the idea of ​​creating a more **genuine and minimalist** space, where the focus is on the experience and not on metrics.
Here, the content speaks for itself — without the pressure of numbers.

---

## &#x1F680; Features

- &#x1F517; **Button of Share**  
  Can share the video with facility!

- &#x1F510; **Login and Registration with Firebase**  
  Safe autenticantion and simple registration with the famous Database (The Firebase).
